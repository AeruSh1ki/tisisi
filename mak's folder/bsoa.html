BSOA

Bachelor of Science in Office Administration

Introduction

The Bachelor of Science in Office Administration (BSOA) is a four-year degree program designed to develop students' skills in managing office operations and administrative tasks efficiently.

This program prepares students to become highly organized professionals capable of handling communication, coordination, and management responsibilities in various business environments.

Course Description

BSOA focuses on office management, administrative procedures, business communication, and information management. It also includes training in modern office technologies and systems used in today's workplaces.

Students will gain both theoretical knowledge and practical experience to effectively support business operations and organizational goals.

What You Will Learn

• Office management and organization
• Business communication and correspondence
• Records and information management
• Event planning and coordination
• Use of office software and technology

Skills You Will Gain

• Organizational and multitasking skills
• Communication and interpersonal skills
• Attention to detail
• Time management and efficiency
• Problem-solving and decision-making

Program Features

• Hands-on training in office systems
• Exposure to real office environments
• Internship opportunities
• Training in modern office tools and software
• Professional development activities
