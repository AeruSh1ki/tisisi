BTVTED

Bachelor of Technical-Vocational Teacher Education

Course Introduction

The Bachelor of Technical-Vocational Teacher Education (BTVTED) program prepares students to become competent educators in technical and vocational fields. It combines teaching principles with hands-on technical training to ensure graduates are ready for both the classroom and industry.

This program is ideal for individuals who are passionate about teaching and skilled in technical areas such as electronics, automotive, ICT, or industrial arts. It develops both instructional competence and practical expertise.

Course Description

BTVTED focuses on professional education courses, technical specialization, and competency-based training. Students learn how to design lessons, conduct skills training, and assess learners based on industry standards.

The program also includes practicum experiences where students teach in real classroom or workshop settings to gain actual teaching experience.

What You Will Learn

• Teaching strategies for technical education
• Skills training and workshop management
• Curriculum and instructional material development
• Competency-based assessment methods
• Safety practices in technical environments

Skills You Will Gain

• Technical and vocational expertise
• Communication and mentoring skills
• Classroom and workshop management
• Critical thinking and problem-solving
